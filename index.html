Background

I got my start in product design at Mandala Group, a company I co-founded in China with two highly inspirational individuals. My role was to co-ordinate the product design team in the research phase. This meant spending weeks at a time in rural Hunan, in the center of China.

The job role included interviewing village officals and farmers and synethsizing the teams findings into a research report and design breif. The result was MoFarm.

Quickly I learnt that understanding the problem space is one aspect, and another equally as important was to be able to communicate the problem space to the whole team.

Acknowledging this I have recently undertaken Le Wagon’s full-stack coding bootcamp to bridge my own knowledge gap between tech and design and relocated to Melbourne, Australia where I am seeking a full-time frontend developer opportunity in a mission oriented company.
