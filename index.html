Work

Some projects I have done using React.js include Gify and Catch of The Day. Both are deployed and in operation.

With focus on different aspects of UX, I have completed the following pieces of work. MoFarm: Rural China research guide, Mirror Fashion and a teardown of Envato Tuts+. These three projects emphasize my thought process and UX principal application.

I’ve also helped build a few WeChat Mini Programs, most notably MindRock.

Currently I am working on a personal project called theUXof.xyz. A series of teardowns that explore the user experience of individuals, businesses and products and what makes them delightful (or not!).

Background

I got my start in product design at Mandala Group, a company I co-founded in China with two highly inspirational individuals. During my time at Mandala Group I undertook two key roles. The first was programs co-ordinator. We ran 4-week idea incubation programs that allowed entrepreneurs, designers and coders from all across the world to come to China and work on one of our current projects.

The second role was product designer. Leading a small team of researchers and designers through rural Hunan, we spent weeks at a time collecting data, interviewing villagers and synthesizing these trips into tangible design briefs.

As a group we focused heavily on finding solutions for the Left Behind Children issue. The result was MoFarm.

Quickly I learnt that understanding the problem space is one aspect, but being able to clearly communicate the problem space to the development team was equally as important.

Acknowledging this I have recently undertaken Le Wagon’s full-stack coding bootcamp to bridge my own knowledge gap between tech and design.

I amn currently based in Melbourne, Australia where I am seeking a full-time frontend developer position in a mission oriented company.

I'm also available for React.js projects and to build sweet little one pager websites like this one.
